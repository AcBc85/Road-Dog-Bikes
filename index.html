Road Dog Bikes

Get the bike you want and find the road that's calling you!

Road Dog Bike's is a dealer for S&M, Kink, Sunday, Verde , DK and more. Why would you come to Road Dog Bike's? That’s a question we ask ourselves every morning when we punch in. For a small, independently-owned shop in any industry to survive the age of Amazon, something must be separating us from the rest of the walruses on the beach.

For us, the beauty of a bike is that it’s not just a fun toy or a stunning display of modern technology, it’s a magnifier of the human experience. Bikes draw us out of ourselves, out into the world and among the fabric of our communities, be it in town, on a track or down the trail.
